# Niches

## Primary Niche: Real Estate

Real estate agents and inmobiliarias receive repeated WhatsApp questions about price, location, availability, requirements, visits, and similar properties.

Useful qualifying questions:

- Are you looking to buy or rent?
- Which zone are you interested in?
- What is your approximate budget?
- What type of property are you looking for?
- How soon do you want to move or buy?
- Do you want to schedule a visit?

Human handoff should happen when a lead has budget, zone, property type, and intent to visit or continue.

## Secondary Niches

- Retail and clothing stores: stock, sizes, delivery, payment, pickup.
- Clinics and psychology practices: availability, service type, appointment intent, urgency.
- Veterinary clinics: service need, pet type, urgency, appointment scheduling.
- Beauty salons and barbershops: service, preferred time, location, booking intent.

## Positioning

Sell outcomes:

- faster replies
- fewer lost leads
- organized leads
- automatic follow-up
- clear handoff to a human
